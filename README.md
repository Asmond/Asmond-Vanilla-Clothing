# Asmond Vanilla Clothing

**Asmond – Vanilla Clothing** adds **800+ custom-made retextures** to DayZ vanilla clothing, providing greater visual diversity and loot customization while preserving the original vanilla experience.

All retextures are designed to integrate seamlessly into the base game, making them suitable for both players and server owners looking for more variety without breaking immersion.

---

## 🎯 Design Philosophy

- The mod is intended to look and feel **100% vanilla-friendly**.

- Existing camouflage patterns have been expanded and adapted.

- No original vanilla clothing item has been modified or replaced.

- “New” clothing pieces are created using models already present in the game.

- You won’t find fluorescent clothing or unrealistic seasonal combinations.

- You won’t find overpowered gear such as 200-slot backpacks or indestructible vests.

- Insulation, weight, capacity values, damage values, hitpoins... nothing was changed.

- You won’t find logos, symbols, or references related to politics, sexuality, or other immersion-breaking themes.

### 📌 Goal

Enrich the vanilla experience without altering the original design philosophy of DayZ.
